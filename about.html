About Pollweb

Welcome to Pollweb, your go-to platform for creating and participating in online polls and surveys. Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and versatile polling experience for individuals and organizations alike.

At Pollweb, we believe in the power of data and the importance of gathering opinions and feedback from a diverse audience. Whether you're looking to gather insights for research, make decisions as a team, or simply have fun with polls, Pollweb has you covered.

Key features of Pollweb include:

  • Easy-to-create polls and surveys
  • Customizable poll options
  • Real-time polling results
  • Secure and anonymous voting
  • Integration options for websites and social media

We're committed to continually improving Pollweb based on your feedback and needs. Join our growing community of users and start creating, sharing, and analyzing polls today!
