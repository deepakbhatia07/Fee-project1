Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a Poll?
A: To create a Poll, log in to your PollWeb account, click on the "+Create a Poll Question" button, and follow the step-by-step instructions in the survey builder.

Q: Can I customize the appearance of my polls?
A: Not now, that will be provided with the prime membership of Pollweb which we will be updating soon.

Q: How do I analyze Poll responses?
A: You can analyze Poll responses Just by submitting your poll. It provides visualizations and insights into the collected data.
Q: How can I share my Poll responses
A: Just after submitting your poll, results will be visualised to you with some sharing buttons of social media from where you can easily share your responses
